About Angelo

Angelo Tadeo creates works that puts fun and interactivity first before anything else. He works in both digital and physical mediums, though most often does digital illustrations. Angelo likes to put emphasis on color in his works, making sure that the combinations in the works feels harmonious while fitting in the message he wants to convey to his viewers. While Angelo is mainly a digital illustrator, he is not afraid to venture off into different mediums of art, working with textiles to clay, to even steel sculptures.
